Credit Card Fraud Detection

February 2022 - March 2022

Python/R/RapidMiner, Data Mining Group Project

Four different classification models built to compare the efficiency results of detecting fraudulent credit card purchases. The dataset was taken from a Kaggle competition. We had to pre-process the data by undersampling due to the large imbalance of data skewing towards non-fraudulent purchases. Then the K-Nearest Neighbor, Decision Tree, Random Forest, and Naïve Bayes classification models were implemented to test against the processed dataset to compare results in accuracy, precision, and recall.
